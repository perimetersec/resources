## About Gianluca Brigandi

Gianluca Brigandi is a Tooling Specialist at Perimeter.

He is focused on custom analysis workflows Web3 systems, especially L2, L1 and L0 infrastructure. His work combines compiler construction, program analysis, fuzzing, and AI-assisted triage to surface issues that generic tools often miss.

He is the author of [Traverse](https://traverse.tools), a Solidity analysis suite for call-graph, storage, and testing workflows. He also created [Tameshi](https://tameshi.dev), a real-time audit-as-you-code scanner with VSCode integration. In support of that work, he built [ThalIR](https://github.com/tameshi-dev/thalir), an SSA-based intermediate representation for EVM security analysis.

His approach centers on building purpose-fit tooling, including differential fuzzers, static analyzers, and triage pipelines, to find deep-rooted and novel bugs. He has multiple confirmed bounty findings.

Before web3, Gianluca spent decades building cybersecurity and infrastructure software, with deep experience across identity, access management, and security orchestration. He co-founded Atricore and led R&D on enterprise identity products, including JOSSO, an open-source SSO platform. His earlier work also included systems used by Fortune 500 companies and government clients.
